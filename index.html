    Search for books related to your topic of interest using the middlesex Library.
  • "Google Books on [topic]"
    Search for books related to your topic of interest using Google Books.
  • "Show me reading sources"
    Request the reading list specifically related to third-year Computer Science .
  • "Open Link [Corresponding Link Number]"
    Open a hyperlink to access the reading list resources. Say [Corresponding Link Number] with the actual number of the link provided.
  • "Libraries near me"
    Find libraries located near your current geographical position on a map.
  • "Close map"
    Close the map view and return to the main chat interface.
  • "Ask OpenAI"
    Pose a question to the OpenAI's language model for a text-based answer within the chat interface by including "Ask OpenAI in your sentence".
